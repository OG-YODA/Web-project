Porsche Carrera

◀
▶

The Porsche Carrera (911) is a sports car (G-segment) that has been in production since 1963. It is one of the most famous in automotive history and one of the three oldest sports cars still in production. The 911 replaced the 356 model in the brand's lineup. At first, the Porsche 911 was to be numbered 901, but in the face of opposition from Peugeot, which reserved three-digit numbers with a zero in the middle, the name was changed to 911. Despite the passage of time, successive generations of the Porsche 911 stylistically have not changed significantly, making the 911 one of the most distinctive and desirable cars in its class. The new generation of the model (designated 991) was launched in 2011.
